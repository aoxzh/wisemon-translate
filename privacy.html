Privacy Policy

Wisemon sends text only to the translation API endpoint you configure. The extension does not operate a relay server.

Stored Data

  • API settings and keys are stored in browser extension storage.
  • Translation cache and logs are stored locally in the browser.
  • You can export, import, clear cache, and clear logs from Settings.

Sensitive Data Masking

When privacy masking is enabled, emails, phone numbers, bank card numbers, and common key or secret patterns are replaced with placeholders before translation requests and restored after the response.

Network Requests

Translation requests go directly from your browser to your configured provider, such as DeepSeek, OpenAI, Ollama, or a custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint.
